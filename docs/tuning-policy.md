# Tuning policy

Tuning is scoped to the narrowest stable entity and signal combination. Process names, tools such as PowerShell/curl, countries and broad network ranges are not sufficient exclusion keys on their own.

Every exception records the rule, exact scope, business justification, owner role, creation and expiry dates, rollback condition and regression case. Multi-signal attack chains must remain alertable when only one signal is trusted. Expired exceptions fail review and must be removed or explicitly renewed after evidence review.

The lab does not claim measured false-positive reduction. A deployment decision would additionally require baseline volume, affected population, expected reduction and post-change monitoring.
